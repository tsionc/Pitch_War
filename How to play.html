" Rules of the Game"

Once game starts players will hear 10 seconds of a note. After the note stops playing players must press on there button to try to answer. Who ever presses there button will have 7 seconds to answer the question. If they guess the right note thre score will increse. However, if the guess wrong the other player will get a chance to answer the question. if the second player guess right. Then, the first player will lose a point.
